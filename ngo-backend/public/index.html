Road Safety Initiative — Our Signature Project

No helmet? No licence? Instead of a fine, we offer a fix — a helmet at challan cost and instant learning-licence support, right at the checkpoint.

→
The signature project

From punishment
to prevention.

Most riders stopped without a helmet or licence walk away with a fine and no safer than before. We partner with local authorities to offer something better at the point of check: the safety gear and the paperwork help they actually needed.

It costs the same as the challan. It changes what happens next.
